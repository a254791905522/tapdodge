Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Stop the Bar – Perfect Timing ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles user information. We built this App with a privacy-first approach and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

No Data Collection: Stop the Bar – Perfect Timing does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The developer does not collect any data from this app.

Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be completely transparent. Our App does not collect any of the following:

Any game-related data such as your personal best scores and game preferences are stored exclusively on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Stop the Bar – Perfect Timing does not integrate third-party analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or social media SDKs. We do not serve ads within the App, and no third-party service has access to your data through our App.

Children's Privacy

No special parental consent is needed because no data collection occurs.

6

Data Security

Because we do not collect or store any user data on our servers, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your personal information through our App. Your game data resides solely on your personal device, protected by your own device security measures.
